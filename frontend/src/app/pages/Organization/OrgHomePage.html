About BackPack Beginnings

Founded in 2010, BackPack Beginnings is a volunteer-driven organization dedicated to delivering child-centric services to feed, comfort, and clothe children in need. We serve over 20,000 children annually in Guilford County, North Carolina, through our food pantries, comfort programs, clothing pantries, and personal care programs.
